# Cancer-Care
Bridging the Cure Divide: Transforming Cancer Care in Pakistan

Cancer is a major public health issue in Pakistan, with an estimated 148,000 new cases diagnosed and nearly 100,000 deaths annually. The most common cancers in Pakistan include:
Breast: The most common cancer overall, accounting for 21.4% of cases in both genders combined
Oral: The most common cancer in males, accounting for 11.6% of cases
Colorectal: A common cancer in both males and females
Prostate: A common cancer in males, with prevalence increasing significantly in recent years
Non-Hodgkin lymphoma: A common cancer in both males and females
